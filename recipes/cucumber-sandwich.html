Cucumber Sandwich

Refreshing cucumber sandwiches for any occasion!

One of my favorite foods that can be eaten as a meal with a side or as a standalone snack.

Ingredients:

  • Cucumbers sliced
  • Your favorite bread (Mine is sourdough)
  • Mayonnaise or Dijon Mustard

Steps:

  1. Spread mayonnaise or mustard on each slice of bread. If you're using mustard, make sure to not add too much as the flavor can be overpowering
  2. Carefully stack desired amount of cucumbers onto the bread.
  3. Close sandwich and enjoy!
