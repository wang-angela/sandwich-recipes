Tomato Sandwich

Classic tomato sandwich for summer!

An old comfort food for me to enjoy. It's very important to get the best tomatoes to get the best sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes sliced
  • Your favorite bread (Mine is sourdough)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Spread mayonnaise on each slice of bread.
  2. Carefully stack desired amount of tomatoes onto the bread.
  3. Top with salt and freshly ground pepper.
  4. Close sandwich and enjoy!
