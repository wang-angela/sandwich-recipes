Toast Sandwich

Budget toast sandwich for those money conscious people!

I don't really enjoy toast sandwiches, but they are good when you're on a budget.

Ingredients:

  • Three slices of bread
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Butter both sides of a slice of bread.
  2. Toast buttered bread in a skillet.
  3. Place toasted bread in between the other two slices of bread.
